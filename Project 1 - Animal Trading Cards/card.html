Agent P

Perry is the pet platypus of the Flynn-Fletcher family, and is perceived as mindless and domesticated. In secret, however, he lives a double life as a member of an all-animal espionage organization referred to as O.W.C.A. (Organization Without a Cool Acronym).

  • Original name: Perry the Platypus
  • Alias: Agent P
  • Gender: Male
  • Occupation: Espionage

Perry the Platypus, also known as Agent P or simply Perry, is an anthropomorphic platypus from the American animated series Phineas and Ferb. Perry was created by the series' co-founders, Dan Povenmire and Jeff "Swampy" Marsh.
